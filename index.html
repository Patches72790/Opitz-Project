Opitz Management, Inc. has been caring for a variety of properties since 1964. We manage each property like we own it and live in it ourselves.
Opitz Management, Inc. is a full-service Property Management Company. We can assist you with finding the perfect home or office space. We can also manage your rental or investment property. Our experienced team offers easy and hassle-free rental, management and 24 hour maintenance services.
